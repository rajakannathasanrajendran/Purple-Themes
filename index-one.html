About Sidelines

Experience the Difference

At Sidelines, we take pride in offering fantastic food, drinks, and sports. With a variety of domestic and local beers on tap and more than 20 big-screen TVs, we’ve created the perfect place to enjoy a game or hang out with friends and family!

Our menu is packed with delicious options to satisfy any craving—from mouthwatering burgers and sizzling wings to fresh salads and shareable appetizers. Every bite is made with care, so you’ll love every dish we serve.

Whether you’re here for the food, the drinks, the game, or the good times, we’re thrilled to have you as part of the Sidelines family. Come in, grab a seat, and enjoy the ultimate sports bar experience today!
